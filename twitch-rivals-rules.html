Twitch Rivals Rules v2

The scoring system has been updated, after the first Twitch Rivals US tournament. The EU teams will be the first ones competing with this format. The new scoring system seems more balanced than the previous one in which games with more rounds were favored. Guess what? Now games with less rounds are favored. But at least with the new format skilled players are awarded. The maximum number of points a team can acquire per game is 9. Everything else remains the same.

FORMAT

  • 80 Invited Streamers split into 20 Teams of 4
  • Teams compete in up to 10 games of Fall Guys
  • Teams will be ranked and receive prizing based on their final standings

RULES

  • Teams will only queue for a total of 6 regulation games + 4 knockout games
  • After the 7th game (Knockout Game 1), 50% of the teams (11th-20th place) are knocked out
  • After the 8th game (Knockout Game 2), 50% of the remaining teams (6th-10th place) are knocked out
  • After the 9th game (Knockout Game 3), 50% of the remaining teams (3rd-5th place) are knocked out
  • After the 10th game (Knockout Game 4), the team in 2nd place is knocked out

SCORING

  • For every team member that reaches the Final Round of a Show: +1 Point
  • For any team member that wins the Final Round of a Show: +5 Points

PRIZING

  • 1st: $7,000
  • 2nd: $4,000
  • 3rd - 5th: $3,000
  • 6th - 10th: $2,500
  • 11th - 15th: $2,000
  • 16th - 20th: $1,500
